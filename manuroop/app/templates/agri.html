AGRI-PRODUCTS CRM

Situation

Nuziveedu Seeds Ltd was looking for a MobileApplication to enable and automate the manual process of Sales transactions and enhance communication through digital visibility by developing the Web based PHP/Android Mobile Application.

Solution

The solution is PHP Based Web portal and Android Mobile, this App is easy to Customize, affordable and user-friendly responsive Web/Mobile Application from Pegasys. It had all the features Nuziveedu Seeds Ltd requires and can be customized to the micro level requirements

Following are the key benefits of our solution

To monitor Field staff or employees of NSL and register attendance on their daily schedule of activities

  1. To monitor product movement and ensure traceability across the channel
  2. MIS dashboard for Field staff activities with an API to HRIS application.
  3. For initiation of online sales transactions through sales order generation at the retailer or distributor point. The online sales orders to directly or indirectly sync with the ERP/SAP based Sales indent system of NSL.
  4. Forms for collection of market data on set parameters which will serve as inputs for Market intelligence dashboard.
  5. Customizable inbuilt Forms feature for configurability and flexibility of using the application by required customization for meeting the user needs of marketing, sales, production and Product evaluation functions.
  6. Role based access, standard user authentication& security features.
